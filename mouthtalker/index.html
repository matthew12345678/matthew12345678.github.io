Puppet Rig

Load a face photo, let MediaPipe find the eyes and mouth, then drive a crude jaw-drop puppet: blink on demand, or feed in a speech clip and watch the jaw open with it. MediaPipe only knows human faces — for an animal (or if detection misses), switch on Edit landmarks and drag the dots onto its eyes and mouth yourself.
